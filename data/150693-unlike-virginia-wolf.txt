I am quiet


So be it


I am tranquil


So be it


I am different


So be it


I am hurt


So be it


I am naïve


So be it


I am full of hope


So be it


 


Fully self-affirmed


Well, not…


but at least trying


Writing poetry


with blue-brocated nails


Unlike Virginia Wolf
