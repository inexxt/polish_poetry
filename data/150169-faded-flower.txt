I am a dried faded flower


sitting on the window-sill


so easy to fall out – yet, I’m still


in my vase of solitary confusion


Am I through?


Or may someone like me, dried-up as I am?


Wouldn’t like to find myself stored


between the pages of a too-hard-to-finish book


or scaterred in pieces on the floor


It might be better to take the plunge now, after all
